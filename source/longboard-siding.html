Longboard siding

Longboard siding made a revolution in the area of house renovations. It’s a very beautiful covering, which offers a wide range of benefits to landowners. The material for cladding is produced out of extruded aluminum, which provides the basic qualities of the covering. The anodized finish & powder coating add extra protection.

The surface is created to imitate natural wood without any cold metallic look. Longboard covering’s a wonderful solution for both commercial & residential buildings. The cladding system meets the AAMA 509-9 Rainscreen certification, which adds to its reliability. 

Pros & Cons of the Covering

The Aluminum Longboard Siding possesses the following advantages:

  • A very nice & pleasant look
  • Impressive durability (about 50 years)
  • Absolute resistance to rot, wrapping, and fading under the influence of sunlight
  • Light weight
  • The comfort of installation
  • A wide choice of textures & shades are available
  • Resistance to corrosion
  • Versatility
  • Eco-friendliness & recyclability
  • Minimal maintenance (only regular washing)
  • Fire resistance
  • Thermal efficiency
  • Protection from wind
  • Fast setting up
  • Resistance to any weather conditions

The following disadvantages of longboard siding Canada should be considered as well::

  • High price compared to some other types of covering
  • Professional installation is required.

Variations of Covering

The varieties of styles available with such covering are not numerous. Still, they allow creating unique designs and successfully combine the material with other types of cladding. Let’s look at the main profiles used for longboard covering:

  • V-Groove

    It will become a wonderful & versatile solution for both facades & longboard soffits. The length of the panels is usually 24 ft. However, it’s possible to order customized sizes. This variety is suitable for any type of building. The planks are easy to install. In addition, it’s possible to set up them either horizontally or vertically. Thanks to the advanced tongue & groove system, the longboard panels are quickly installed with quick screen clips. The method presupposes a hidden allocation of elements, so the surface will be smooth & neat. V-Groove panels are produced in various sizes — the width from 10 to 15 cm and the longboard siding thickness is from 1.57 to 1.78 mm.

  • Channel

    The length of such profiles is 24 ft as well. They are produced with a trim system that includes corners, j-channels, and other elements for easy installation. The peculiarity of such longboard aluminum siding profiles is that the profile of each board slightly overlaps the next one. Thus, a shadow is created, which helps achieve an interesting visual effect. The panels can also be installed in any direction. To create a special effect, it’s also possible to install the panels with certain seams. Thus, the facade of the house can become very nice & prominent.

The longboard Calgary series offers not only standard panels, which look like the majority of other solutions for house renovations. The manufacturer also offers a unique system of Panelboards, which allows using specifically designed planks & elements in order to create an impression of a solid large-format panel. Thus, we can enjoy more flexibility compared to the installation of real large panels. The material is perfect for longboard inspiring facades & longboard soffit installation.

Prices

The cost of longboard siding can be different because various issues can influence the price. The following aspects can make it either higher or lower:

  • The footage of the house
  • The type of profile selected
  • The complexity of preparation works
  • The need for repairs
  • The necessity to remove the old covering
  • The location of the house.

If you want to use customized solutions in terms of sizes, colors, or other aspects, the longboard siding price will be higher.
In general, the average longboard siding prices start from 45 USD for one square foot. It means that for the house with footage of 1,800 sq. ft, it will be necessary to pay at least 81,000 USD.
