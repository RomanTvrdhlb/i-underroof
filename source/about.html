About us

  • Our Journey in Alberta

    Since its inception in 2002, I UNDERROOF has deeply rooted itself in the rich soils of Alberta, establishing a bond with the local community that only grows stronger with time. We began as a small team with a grand vision: to redefine the essence of construction by intertwining quality craftsmanship with the warm embrace of Canadian hospitality. Today, with every brick we lay and every home we build, we're not just constructing structures but building lifelong relationships.

  • The I UNDERROOF Philosophy

    As we embarked on our journey, we realized that the foundation of a successful construction project isn't just materials and labor—it's trust, understanding, and mutual respect. At I UNDERROOF, we've always believed in maintaining transparency with our clients, offering them insights and involving them in every step of the construction journey. This philosophy has transformed our clients into our family, making every project a collaborative venture filled with shared dreams and aspirations.

  • Building Dreams, One Project at a Time

    At I UNDERROOF, we're more than just a construction company; we're dream-weavers. Whether it's a cozy family home nestled in the picturesque landscapes of Alberta or a modern commercial complex designed to make a statement, we bring visions to life with unmatched precision and dedication. Our team, a blend of experienced veterans and enthusiastic young talent, is driven by a singular passion—to craft spaces that resonate with emotions, aspirations, and memories. Because at UNDERROOF, we don't just build structures; we create homes where dreams thrive.
