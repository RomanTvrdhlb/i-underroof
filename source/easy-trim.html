EasyTrim

EasyTrim Quick Panel is possibly one of the most comprehensive systems invented for cladding. The material is represented by an aluminum composite created out of coil-coated metal with a mineral-filled fire-resistant core or polyethylene.

Such a solution allows achieving a truly clean and stylish result of house renovation works. The final result can turn out to be a state of art thanks to the unique designs, which might be created with EasyTrim Reveals. The manufacturer offers a full range of profiles and elements to find a perfect solution for each house.

Pros & Cons of the Covering

The system offers the following benefits:

  • Light weight
  • The ease of Easy Trim installation
  • Sturdiness
  • Incredible protection from wind and other weather conditions
  • Compatibility with various siding solutions (including cement fiberboard siding, natural wood, etc.)
  • Improved durability thanks to the use of 6063-T5 aluminum
  • Resistance to sunlight
  • Eco-friendliness and recyclability
  • The ease of water management
  • Minimal maintenance
  • Absolute fire resistance with the corresponding certificates
  • A complete set of tools and materials for installation
  • Any color variation accessible due to an opportunity to paint the material
  • The versatility of the covering for both residential and commercial buildings.

QuickPanel Systems have only a few drawbacks:

  • It should be installed by professionals in order to avoid mistakes
  • The cost of the siding system might be higher compared to certain alternatives.

Variations of Covering

The Ez Trim Quick Panel system was developed for the fast installation of 4 mm ACM panels. Thanks to the fact that the system for siding is impressively customizable, it’s possible to create any sort of design. Besides, all the works can be fulfilled right on-site. Due to this, it’s possible to reduce expenses and do everything much faster compared to other types of siding. The maximum size of a panel available is 13 cm x 21 cm. The maximum length of Ez Trim profiles is 25.5 cm. The panels and profiles can be cut to perfectly fit the surface. Depending on the place and purpose, the manufacturer offers all types of profiles for the creation of a smooth design:

  • One- and two-piece square corner profiles
  • Rounded corner profiles
  • Arrow corner profiles
  • Multi-angle corner profiles
  • Vertical trims of various types
  • Profiles for window flashings
  • Horizontal panel trims
  • Soffit trims, and other profiles for the creation of a comprehensive siding of the building.

All trims get primed after the production to guarantee durability and excellent characteristics. Thanks to the diversity of profiles and their sizes, it’s possible to realize any project. The following options are available with Easy Trim:

  1. Lap design of the aluminum siding Canada. It’s possible to easily install any sort of panels you like to provide a wonderful look of the house together with high protection from wind, rain, and other weather conditions.

  2. Board and batten. It’s another popular design, which is able to make any house stand out. The seams between wider residential or commercial aluminum siding panels will be covered by narrow strips, which prevent water from getting under the covering.

  3. Vertical panels. Thanks to the presence of the corresponding profiles, it’s possible to realize the designs with vertical planks made of any material.

With such a system, perfect symmetry might be achieved. Besides, no finishing works will be required because of the availability of ready-made profiles, which make the look complete right after the panels installation. Note that the system is compatible with cedar panel siding and many other sorts of covering.
