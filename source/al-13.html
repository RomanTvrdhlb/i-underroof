AL-13

AL-13 refers to the metal siding types. Due to its visual look & grade, such material is more typical for commercial buildings. However, homeowners who would like to give their houses a contemporary image frequently choose such siding.

The material is represented by the composite with a multi-layered structure and aluminum basis.Either polyethylene or fire-resistant mineral fiber might be used as a core. Thanks to the protective finish, the covering is resistant to various weather conditions. The AL13 siding is created with the help of a tab-over system, which applies snap-lock fasteners.

Pros & Cons of the Covering

AL13 offers a wide range of advantages to landowners::

  • Fast installation
  • Resistance to temperature changes (the material is able to expand & contract to adapt)
  • Fire resistance
  • Durability
  • Wind & water resistance
  • Minimum maintenance (all you might need to do is to clean the surface)
  • Versatility
  • The ease of installation
  • Numerous finish options are available (including popular colors & textures)
  • Modern & stylish look
  • Absolute protection from pests
  • Eco-friendliness & recyclability.

There’re also a few disadvantages to consider:

  • Scratches & dents might appear on the surface
  • Higher metal siding prices compared to many other types of siding.

Variations of Covering

With AL13, it’s possible to create unique & extremely beautiful designs for houses and commercial buildings. This material is produced in two variations:

  • AL13 panels

    It’s a very comfortable solution that allows realizing any design. The material can be comfortably cut on-site to perfectly fit the walls. The exterior metal siding panels can be easily installed on any type of structure without prefabrication. It’s a perfect solution for any climate because the panels can easily adapt to extreme changes in temperature. It’s possible to install metal building siding panels in any direction — vertically or horizontally. As a result, there’s nothing difficult in the creation of extraordinary styles.

The planks are created to imitate natural wood, which adds a very authentic look. The covering of such a type will never sag or crack. The material might be used not just for walls but also for soffits. This variety of covering is the main competitor of Longboard metal siding. The planks can also be installed either vertically or horizontally. For a more unique design, it’s possible to combine various techniques.

Colors

The varieties of hues with AL 13 Panels & V-Line covering are almost unlimited. There’s a wide palette of the most suitable and popular colors used for house siding. The collection is divided into several categories, which are:

  • Solid hues. The category is represented by both soothing and bright colors of steel panel siding. It’s possible to choose more conservative black, white, gray, brown, or beige shades. The collection also includes vibrant colors, which are able to make an interesting emphasis — yellow, blue, and red shades.

  • Woodline collection. Those who want to see something natural in their house will definitely find something suitable in this category. The most beautiful & distinctive textures of maples, oaks, teaks, pears, firs, cherries, rosewoods, and walnuts are available to homeowners among the varieties of metal wood siding. Not only textures but also hues are very different.

  • Metallic collection. For something more solid & calm, choose one of the colors from this category. They are represented by several shades of gray with a typical metallic gloss.

Custom colors of metal siding sheets are also possible. To get one, a template will be required. However, it might take more time. All the materials are treated & covered with a corresponding finish right after the production. They are completely ready to create a perfect house design.

Prices

To understand the cost of work, it’s always required to consult us. The price might be different depending on:

  • The size of the house & the amount of work
  • The thickness of the material
  • The finish/color of the AL13 panels
  • The necessity to remove the old covering
  • The complexity of a project
  • The number of cuts required
  • The location of the house
  • The needs for any preparation works.

On average, the cost of AL13 siding Calgary is about 44 USD per square foot. The size of a standard house is 1,800 sq. ft. Thus, to renovate the whole house, you will need to pay 79,200 USD. Keep in mind that depending on the peculiarities of the project, the price might change.
