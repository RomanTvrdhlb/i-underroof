Engineered siding

Engineered siding is one of the varieties of covering, which is considered a perfect alternative to natural wood (cedar covering). The surface is cost-efficient & looks like a natural wood siding. The covering is produced out of composite materials, which includes natural wood and resin.

Wood fibers get covered with special wax for the creation of higher water resistance and the achievement of other valuable qualities. Besides, binders are added to the composite. Finally, the material is heat-pressed to achieve greater characteristics. The covering might also be treated with zinc borate. The final stage is a layer of resin.

Pros & Cons of the Covering

The advantages of the composite wood siding are:

  • A natural & pleasant look
  • Great resistance to moisture, pests, and mold
  • Resistance to significant temperature changes
  • Eco-friendliness (the covering is often produced out of recycled wood and is safe for people & animals)
  • Resistance to various environmental impacts & any weather conditions
  • The ease of setting up
  • Affordability compared to hardwood siding
  • Impressive durability (about 30 years)
  • A diversity of designs, textures, and hues are available
  • Great strength & density.

The disadvantages of imitation wood siding are:

  • Due to cracks, the material might start to absorb moisture, which will lead to swelling/wrapping
  • Regular maintenance & painting are required.

Variations of Covering

There exist various ways to use engineered wood siding. The material might be cut into very different pieces, so landowners can create unique styles for their houses. The following types of such covering might be applied:

  • Lap

    It’s one of the most popular options that can add an elegant & stylish look to any house. The boards are located horizontally while each one overlays the previous one. The widths & textures of faux wood siding panels might be very different, which allows creating unique designs. Along with visual benefits, the covering additionally provides good insulation & protection from winds. The covering is durable & comfortable to take care of.

  • Vertical engineered wood siding panels

    Such sort of covering can actually be installed in any direction, which makes it a versatile solution. Compared to lap covering, this variety is shorter and, thus, more flexible to work with. The look of the covering is very nice & elegant. This type might be used individually or in a composition with lap covering in order to add certain accents to the general style of the house. Besides, it’s possible to use panels with various widths & designs.

  • Shake & shingles exterior faux wood siding

    It’s another popular sort of covering, which can be made with the material. The style adds a certain warmth & cozy look to any building. It’s required to understand the difference. While shingles look cleaner & more up-to-date thanks to the creation of a smooth surface, shakes are a bit more ‘messy’ and can have various sizes with chaotic surfaces. As a result, shakes make a more rustic look of the house. In addition, an attractive shadow effect is created. The planks for such wood sidings Canada are usually thick enough to guarantee durability & strength.

  • Board & batten

    It’s a popular & easy-to-install variety. In this case, wider panels are spaced apart with narrow strips. As a result, it’s possible to create a distinctive & very interesting pattern. Such style guarantees exceptional insulation from wind and other weather impacts. Engineered board and batten siding is a very practical solution in terms of maintenance & timeless choice in terms of its design. The covering can come with various finishes in accordance with your taste.

Along with the described varieties, engineered wood covering provides more uniqueness due to the availability of trims & accessories, which can make any house prominent. The covering can be easily customized. There’re various shutters, vents, and other elements that can add completeness to the general design of the building.

Prices

To understand the cost of wood siding installation, it’s required to know the main aspects that can influence the price. They are:

  • The footage of the house
  • The selected wood siding options
  • The need for repairs
  • The complexity of preparation works (old coverage removal & others).

The average price for one square foot is from 5 USD to 15 USD. Thus, if we take the standard footage of the house, which is 1,800 sq. ft, the total cost will be from 9,000 USD to 27,000 USD. The range of prices is great, so it’s always better to consult the installation contractors.
